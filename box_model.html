BOX MODEL

The CSS Box Model is essentially a box that wraps around every HTML element.

It consists of : Margin, Borders, Padding and the actual content.

The box model allows us top add border around elements , and to define space between elements.

The actual content appears here which could be the required images and texts.

Margin

The outermost area is the margin that is the area outside orange border and inside black border.

Border

The orange region is border, and it is border for the green region.

Padding

Inside the green region the cleared region around the written content is the padding for the content.

Content

The written content is serving as an example of content here, it may contain texts , images, etc.
